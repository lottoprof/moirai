Beginner is Moirai’s entry-level programme. It introduces the language of
cinema through practice and small exercises. By the end, the student has
several short video studies and a working understanding of composition,
editing, and screenwriting.

Each module with homework ends with a 1:1 session with an instructor
(50 minutes): review of the work, a deeper look into the next topic, and
a small exercise.
