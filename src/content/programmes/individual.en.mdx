The individual programme is for cases where a standard course doesn't fit.
For example, you've already shot something and want to go deeper into
editing only. Or you're working on a specific film and need targeted
modules for it.

How it works:

1. Request a discovery call — a short brief about the task
2. The instructor talks with you and understands the request
3. Assembles a programme from Beginner + Intermediate modules plus rare
   specialised topics not in the standard course
4. Sets the price
5. 1:1 sessions, flexible schedule

No cohort — no need to align with a group.
