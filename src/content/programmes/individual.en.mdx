> **By request only.** The Individual programme is not booked through the
> standard Apply flow. Contact us at hello@moiraionline.pro to start a
> discovery conversation.

## When this is for you

The Individual programme is for cases where a standard cohort doesn't fit:

- You've already shot something and want to go deeper into editing only
- You're working on a specific film and need targeted modules for it
- You want pacing that doesn't match a fixed cohort schedule
- You want a curriculum that mixes modules from across our standard tracks
  with specialised topics not covered in the regular course

## How it works

1. Email hello@moiraionline.pro with a short brief about your task
2. The instructor talks with you — a discovery call to understand the request
3. Assembles a programme from Beginner + Intermediate modules plus rare
   specialised topics not in the standard course
4. Sets the price based on scope
5. 1:1 sessions, flexible schedule — no cohort, no need to align with a group

## Not the same as a small standard cohort

If a standard Beginner or Intermediate cohort happens to fill with just
1–2 students, the format becomes effectively 1:1 — but the curriculum and
schedule are still the **standard programme**. The Individual programme
is different: a **custom curriculum** built for your specific need, not
the standard tracks delivered in a small group.
