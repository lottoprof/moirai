Anastasia teaches the Scriptwriting and Producing tracks on Moirai's
Beginner and Intermediate programmes. She works as a filmmaker in
New York and brings a hands-on perspective from active production —
budgets, locations, actor direction, and the script work that ties
everything together.

Her teaching focus: how a story works on the page first, then how it
holds together on set. Each cohort ends with a finished short — built
through individual feedback, not lectures.
