Anastasia holds a film degree in directing and built her career from
the inside out — working as First Assistant Director on television
series, short films, and commercial productions before stepping fully
behind the camera. Her short films have placed at international film
festivals. She specializes in two things most filmmaking courses skip:
the director's script as a working document, and the psychology of
directing actors — how to build trust on set and get a real
performance out of a real person. In the US, she teaches film and
theater, bringing the same approach to every class: precise, human,
and practical.
