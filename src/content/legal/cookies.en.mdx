## 1. What are cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a
website. They allow the site to recognize your browser, maintain a
login session, and remember preferences.

This policy also covers similar technologies (localStorage,
sessionStorage, indexedDB) used by the Moirai site.

## 2. Categories of cookies we use

### Strictly necessary (always on)

| Name | Purpose | Retention |
|---|---|---|
| `__Host-moirai_refresh` | Authentication (refresh session) | 1 day / 7 days |
| `cf_clearance` | Cloudflare bot protection | 30 days |
| Turnstile widget data | Anti-bot verification | session |

These cookies cannot be disabled — the service does not function
without them.

### Preference (opt-in)

| Name | Purpose | Retention |
|---|---|---|
| `moirai_locale` | Language preference (en / ru) | 12 months |

### Analytics

We currently **do not use** analytics cookies. If we add Plausible /
similar privacy-friendly analytics in the future, this section will be
updated and consent will be requested per [Privacy Policy](/en/legal/privacy).

### Marketing / advertising

We do not use marketing or third-party advertising cookies. No data is
shared with ad networks.

## 3. Third-party cookies

Cookies set by services we integrate:

- **Cloudflare** (hosting, security): `cf_clearance`, `__cf_bm`
- **Cloudflare Turnstile** (anti-bot): widget-local cookies, no cross-site tracking
- **Google** / **Discord** (only if you sign in via OAuth): provider cookies on their domains

## 4. Managing cookies

Most browsers allow you to:

- View stored cookies
- Block specific or all cookies
- Delete cookies on browser close

Note: blocking strictly necessary cookies will break login / session
functionality.

Browser-specific instructions:
- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/HT201265)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/help/4027947)

## 5. Do Not Track (DNT)

We honor the DNT browser signal where it does not break required
functionality (auth cookies remain regardless).

## 6. Changes

This policy is updated when cookie inventory changes. The version is
shown at the top of this page.
