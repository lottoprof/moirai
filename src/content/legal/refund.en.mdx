## 1. Scope

This Refund Policy covers refund requests for online courses purchased
from Moirai (moiraionline.pro).

## 2. EU 14-day right of withdrawal

If you are a consumer in the European Union, you have the right to
withdraw from the contract within **14 calendar days** of purchase,
**without giving any reason**, under Directive 2011/83/EU.

**Exception**: Once you start consuming the digital content (e.g.
watch a recording, attend a live session, submit homework), you
expressly waive the withdrawal right by such consumption — per Art. 16(m)
of the Directive. We display this notice at checkout for explicit consent.

To exercise your right: email hello@moiraionline.pro within 14 days
of purchase, before consuming the content.

## 3. Refunds outside the EU

Outside the EU, refund requests are reviewed case-by-case. As a baseline:

- **Within 14 days of purchase, no content consumed**: full refund
- **Within 14 days, content partially consumed**: 50% refund or store credit
- **After 14 days**: refunds are at our discretion

## 4. Cancellation by Moirai

If we cancel a cohort before it starts (e.g. minimum-students threshold
not met, instructor unavailability), you receive a **100% refund** or
the option to enroll in the next cohort.

## 5. Suspensions for ToS violation

Accounts suspended for material breach of the [Terms of Service](/en/legal/terms)
(e.g. recording redistribution, account sharing, harassment) are
**not eligible** for refund.

## 6. Process

1. Email hello@moiraionline.pro with: order ID, registered email, reason
2. We respond within 5 business days
3. Approved refunds are processed within 14 business days, to the
   original payment method
4. Currency conversion losses (if any) are not refunded

## 7. Disputes

If you disagree with a refund decision, you can:

1. Request internal review (reply to the response email)
2. Escalate to your bank/card issuer (chargeback)
3. EU consumers: use the EU [Online Dispute Resolution platform](https://ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr/)

## 8. Changes

We may update this policy; the version applicable to your purchase is
the version in effect on the date of your purchase. Material changes
to future purchases are announced via email.
