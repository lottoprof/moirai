## 1. Who we are

Moirai Online Filmmaking Program ("Moirai", "we", "us") operates the
website moiraionline.pro and provides online filmmaking courses.

**Data controller:** Moirai Online Filmmaking Program.
**Contact:** hello@moiraionline.pro.

## 2. What data we process about you

### 2.1 Data you provide directly

- **At registration**: email address, password (we store a one-way hash, never the plaintext), display name, language preference (en/ru)
- **At OAuth sign-in (Google/Discord)**: your provider account email and provider user ID — we use these to identify you on return; we do **not** keep your Google or Discord access/refresh tokens after the initial verification
- **In profile / account settings**: any name update, language change, password change
- **At enrollment**: programme purchased; we do **not** process or store your payment card — that is handled entirely by our payment processor (Stripe)
- **In homework submissions**: text, attached files (scripts, videos, images you upload)
- **In communications**: email correspondence with the team

### 2.2 Data generated through your use of the service

- **Authentication sessions**: a random session identifier (in a HttpOnly cookie on your device), the SHA-256 hash of the refresh secret (in our database), expiry timestamp, your user-agent string, and a **hashed** IP address (we never store your plaintext IP)
- **Account roles**: which roles you have (student, instructor, admin) and when each was granted
- **Course progress** (when programmes start): module completion, homework submissions, instructor feedback, last-seen timestamps
- **Audit log**: security-relevant events (login success/fail, password reset, role grant, etc.) with timestamp, hashed IP, user-agent — kept for fraud and abuse investigation

### 2.3 Short-lived technical data

Stored only for the duration of a flow (10 minutes to 1 hour) in our key-value store:

- OAuth PKCE state and verifier (during OAuth flow)
- Email-verification and password-reset tokens
- Rate-limit counters (per IP / per email, for anti-abuse)

### 2.4 Categories we do **not** collect or store

- ❌ Plaintext passwords — we store only a PBKDF2-SHA256 hash with a unique random salt
- ❌ Plaintext IP addresses — we store only a SHA-256 hash (using a server-side salt)
- ❌ Payment card data — Stripe handles this end-to-end; we receive only a transaction reference
- ❌ Google or Discord OAuth tokens — used once during sign-in and discarded
- ❌ Analytics tracking — no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, no marketing pixels
- ❌ Retargeting / third-party advertising cookies
- ❌ Special-category data (race, religion, health, political opinions, sexual orientation, biometric/genetic data)
- ❌ Personal data of minors under 16 (see §9)

### 2.5 Data we do not buy or receive from third parties

We do not buy data from data brokers, ad networks, or marketing lists.
All data about you originates from your direct interaction with our service.

## 3. Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6)

- **Contract performance** — delivering the course you purchased
- **Legitimate interest** — security, fraud prevention, internal analytics
- **Consent** — marketing emails (opt-in), cookies beyond strictly necessary

## 4. Cookies & similar technologies

## 5. Data sharing

We share data with:

- **Cloudflare** — hosting, edge caching, security (Pages, Workers, D1)
- **Resend** — transactional email delivery
- **Google** / **Discord** — only if you sign in via OAuth
- **Cloudflare Turnstile** — anti-bot verification on auth forms

We do not sell or rent your data.

## 6. Data retention

- **Active accounts**: until you delete the account
- **Inactive accounts**: anonymized after 24 months of no activity
- **Audit logs**: 12 months (security, abuse investigation)
- **Email logs**: 6 months

## 7. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to:

- Access your data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict processing
- Data portability (machine-readable export)
- Object to processing
- Withdraw consent

Requests: hello@moiraionline.pro. Response within 30 days.

## 8. International transfers

Data is processed on Cloudflare's global edge network. Cloudflare maintains
standard contractual clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers.

## 9. Children

The service is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect
data from minors.

## 10. Changes to this policy

Material changes are announced via email to registered users at least
30 days before taking effect. The current version is shown at the top of
this page.
