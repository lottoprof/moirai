## The Explication

> The first document you need — before anything else

**Why logistics matter:**  
Every film has two sides — creative vision and the logistics that make it possible.  
The explication aligns everyone before production begins.  
Without it — every department imagines a different film.

---

## Locations

> Find it. Vet it. Lock it. Have a backup.

### Location Scouting
- Always bring your cinematographer.
- Walk every corner.
- Check: room for equipment? Light control? Noise?
- Permit required for public spaces — in most US cities, yes.
- Negotiate with a project overview ready.

---

## Props & Payments

### Props & Costumes
- Find them **before** the shoot. Don't improvise.
- Photograph every costume at the start of each day.
- One person tracks props throughout.

### Payments
- Create a payment sheet before production begins.
- Announce the schedule clearly.
- Follow through without exception.

> Late payment damages trust faster than almost anything.
