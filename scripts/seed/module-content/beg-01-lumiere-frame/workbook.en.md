## Assignment: The Lumière Frame

### Rules

1. **Shoot outdoors.** Filming inside — at home, in an apartment, in an office, etc. — is not permitted.
2. **Static camera only**, preferably on a tripod. Panning, tilting, and changes in angle or frame size are not allowed.
3. **No cuts.** The entire piece must be shot in a single take.
4. **Natural light only.** Street lamps, car headlights, neon signs, and similar light sources are acceptable — any light that is not controlled by the director.
5. **Minimal colour grading** is permitted. A black-and-white version is welcome.
6. **No dialogue as the central focus.** Actors and extras may speak to each other on camera, but their speech should not be the main subject of the exercise.
7. **Music is allowed**, but should be kept to a minimum — all attention should remain on what is happening in the frame.
8. **Runtime: 30 seconds to 1 minute.**
